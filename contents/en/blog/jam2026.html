The theme for the event is: DISCONNECT 🍤⛓️‍💥!

It can mean:

  • Breaking or undoing the connection between two things, for example pulling a plug out of a socket
  • Cutting off the electrical current to something by removing it from its power source
  • An interruption of a service, such as a telephone or internet disconnection
  • A lack of communication or agreement between people, for example a disconnect between voters and politicians
  • And many more related meanings 😉

Interpret it as literally or as loosely as you want! Feel free to discuss ideas in forum or on Discord.

For more ideas see the definitions of disconnect in the free dictionary.
